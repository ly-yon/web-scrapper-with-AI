Return Policy
          
  



We cannot accept wallpaper returns on overages or buyer’s remorse.  All wallpapers are printed to order or are printed in batches (dye lots).If an installer suspects a printing error or the package and rolls come damaged, please contact us right away.  Installers that find a printing error must cease installation right away. Manolo Walls is not liable to compensate the installer for installing paper with printing errors. No damage claims can be made on hung paper. Please inspect all material before cutting and hanging.
